# Company Holiday Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Applies To:** All employees of Meridian Technologies, Inc.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the company-observed holidays and related benefits at Meridian Technologies. Our goal is to support employee well-being by providing adequate time for rest, celebration, and personal observances.

## 2. Company-Observed Holidays (2025)

Meridian Technologies observes the following **11 paid holidays** each calendar year:

| Date | Holiday |
|------|---------|
| January 1 | New Year's Day |
| January 20 | Martin Luther King Jr. Day |
| February 17 | Presidents' Day |
| May 26 | Memorial Day |
| June 19 | Juneteenth |
| July 4 | Independence Day |
| September 1 | Labor Day |
| November 27 | Thanksgiving Day |
| November 28 | Day after Thanksgiving |
| December 24 | Christmas Eve |
| December 25 | Christmas Day |

When a holiday falls on a Saturday, it will be observed on the preceding Friday. When a holiday falls on a Sunday, it will be observed on the following Monday.

## 3. Floating Holidays

In addition to company-observed holidays, all full-time employees receive **2 floating holidays** per calendar year. These may be used for:
- Religious observances not covered by company holidays
- Cultural celebrations
- Personal occasions of significance
- Any other purpose the employee chooses

### 3.1 Floating Holiday Guidelines

- Floating holidays must be requested at least **3 business days** in advance
- They must be used within the calendar year and do not carry over
- Unused floating holidays are forfeited on December 31 and are not paid out
- Part-time employees receive 1 floating holiday per year

## 4. Holiday Pay

### 4.1 Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees receive their regular base pay for each company-observed holiday, regardless of the day of the week it falls on.

### 4.2 Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees receive holiday pay only for holidays that fall on their regularly scheduled workdays, prorated based on their standard hours.

### 4.3 Holiday Premium Pay

Non-exempt employees required to work on a company-observed holiday receive **1.5x their regular hourly rate** for all hours worked, in addition to their holiday pay. Exempt employees required to work on a holiday receive a substitute day off to be used within 30 days.

## 5. Holiday Office Closures

The office is officially closed on all company-observed holidays. Employees should not be expected to check email or messages unless they are in a designated on-call rotation.

### 5.1 Extended Closures

Meridian Technologies observes an extended closure from **December 24 through January 1** each year. Employees use company holidays and 1–2 PTO days to cover this period. The exact schedule is announced by November 1 each year.

## 6. Religious and Cultural Accommodations

Meridian Technologies respects the diversity of its workforce. Employees who observe religious or cultural holidays not covered by the company calendar may:
- Use their floating holidays
- Use PTO days
- Request a schedule swap with their manager (e.g., work on a company holiday and take their observance day off instead)

Managers are encouraged to accommodate such requests whenever operationally feasible.
