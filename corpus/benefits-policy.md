# Employee Benefits Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Applies To:** All eligible employees of Meridian Technologies, Inc.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the benefits package available to eligible employees of Meridian Technologies, designed to support employee health, financial security, and overall well-being.

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees (working 30+ hours per week) are eligible for all benefits listed in this policy. Benefits begin on the **first day of the month following 30 days** of employment.

### 2.2 Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees (working 20–29 hours per week) are eligible for limited benefits:
- Dental and vision insurance (employee-only coverage)
- 401(k) plan
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

### 2.3 Dependents

Eligible dependents include:
- Legal spouse or registered domestic partner
- Children up to age 26 (biological, adopted, stepchildren, or legal dependents)

## 3. Health Insurance

### 3.1 Medical Plans

Meridian Technologies offers three medical plan options:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|------|---------------------------|-------------------------|------------|-------------------|
| PPO Gold | $150 | $450 | $500 | $3,000 |
| PPO Silver | $100 | $300 | $1,500 | $5,000 |
| HDHP + HSA | $60 | $180 | $3,000 | $7,000 |

The company contributes **75%** of the total premium cost. The employee premium amounts shown above reflect the employee's 25% share.

### 3.2 Health Savings Account (HSA)

Employees enrolled in the HDHP receive a company HSA contribution of:
- **$750/year** for individual coverage
- **$1,500/year** for family coverage

Contributions are deposited quarterly. Employees may make additional pre-tax contributions up to IRS limits.

### 3.3 Dental Insurance

Two dental plan options are available:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Annual Maximum |
|------|---------------------------|---------------|
| Basic | $20 | $1,500 |
| Premium | $40 | $3,000 (includes orthodontics) |

Preventive care (cleanings, exams, X-rays) is covered at 100% under both plans.

### 3.4 Vision Insurance

Vision insurance covers:
- Annual eye exam: $10 copay
- Frames: $150 allowance every 24 months
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance every 12 months
- Monthly premium: $10 (employee), $25 (family)

## 4. Retirement Benefits

### 4.1 401(k) Plan

Meridian Technologies offers a 401(k) retirement plan with:
- **Company match**: 100% match on the first 4% of salary, plus 50% match on the next 2% (maximum company contribution: 5% of salary)
- **Vesting schedule**: Company contributions vest 20% per year, fully vested after 5 years
- **Auto-enrollment**: New employees are auto-enrolled at 6% contribution rate (can opt out or adjust)
- **Investment options**: 20+ fund options including target-date funds, index funds, and ESG options
- **Roth 401(k)**: Available for after-tax contributions

### 4.2 Financial Planning

All employees have access to complimentary financial planning sessions with a certified financial planner through our partnership with Fidelity. Sessions can be scheduled quarterly.

## 5. Life and Disability Insurance

### 5.1 Life Insurance

- **Basic life insurance**: 2x annual salary (company-paid, up to $500,000)
- **Supplemental life insurance**: Available for purchase at group rates (up to 5x salary)
- **Spouse/dependent life insurance**: Available for purchase

### 5.2 Short-Term Disability (STD)

- Benefit: 60% of base salary for up to 26 weeks
- Elimination period: 7 calendar days
- Company-paid

### 5.3 Long-Term Disability (LTD)

- Benefit: 60% of base salary up to $10,000/month
- Elimination period: 180 days
- Company-paid

## 6. Parental Leave

### 6.1 Birth Parents

Birth parents are eligible for:
- **12 weeks** of fully paid parental leave
- **4 additional weeks** at 60% pay
- Leave may begin up to 2 weeks before the expected due date

### 6.2 Non-Birth Parents

Non-birth parents (including adoptive and foster parents) are eligible for:
- **8 weeks** of fully paid parental leave
- Leave must be taken within 6 months of birth, adoption, or foster placement

### 6.3 Adoption and Surrogacy Assistance

Meridian Technologies provides up to **$10,000** in financial assistance for adoption or surrogacy expenses per child, up to a lifetime maximum of $25,000.

## 7. Additional Benefits

### 7.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Free, confidential counseling and support services for employees and their families, including:
- Mental health counseling (up to 8 sessions per issue per year)
- Legal consultation
- Financial counseling
- Work-life balance resources

### 7.2 Tuition Reimbursement

Employees with 1+ years of service may receive tuition reimbursement of up to **$5,250/year** for job-related degree programs or certifications. Requires manager and HR approval before enrollment.

### 7.3 Commuter Benefits

Pre-tax commuter benefits for:
- Transit passes: Up to $300/month
- Parking: Up to $300/month

### 7.4 Wellness Program

- Annual wellness reimbursement: **$500** for gym memberships, fitness equipment, or wellness apps
- On-site flu shots and biometric screenings
- Mental health days: 2 additional days per year (separate from PTO)

### 7.5 Employee Discount Program

Discounts on products and services through our partnership with PerkSpot, including technology, travel, entertainment, and automotive.

## 8. Open Enrollment

Benefits elections and changes occur during the annual Open Enrollment period in **November** for the following calendar year. Outside of Open Enrollment, changes are only permitted within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, divorce, loss of other coverage).

## 9. COBRA

Former employees may continue health coverage for up to 18 months under COBRA at their own expense (full premium plus 2% administrative fee).
