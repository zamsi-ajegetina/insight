# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Finance Department
**Applies To:** All employees of Meridian Technologies, Inc.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy provides guidelines for the reimbursement of legitimate business expenses incurred by employees while performing their job duties.

## 2. General Principles

All business expenses must be:
- **Necessary** and directly related to company business
- **Reasonable** in amount given the circumstances
- **Properly documented** with original receipts and a completed expense report
- **Pre-approved** when required (see Section 3)

Employees are expected to exercise good judgment and spend company funds as if they were their own. Extravagant or unnecessary expenses will not be reimbursed.

## 3. Approval Requirements

| Expense Amount | Approval Required |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Under $100    | No pre-approval needed |
| $100–$500     | Direct manager approval |
| $500–$2,000   | Director-level approval |
| $2,000–$5,000 | VP-level approval |
| Over $5,000   | CFO approval |

## 4. Expense Categories and Limits

### 4.1 Meals and Entertainment

| Category | Per-Person Limit |
|----------|-----------------|
| Business lunch (local) | $30 |
| Business dinner (local) | $60 |
| Client entertainment dinner | $100 |
| Team celebration meal | $50 |
| Coffee/snacks meetings | $15 |

Alcohol is reimbursable up to 2 drinks per person at client dinners only. Tips should not exceed 20%.

### 4.2 Travel — Transportation

- **Air travel**: Economy class for flights under 6 hours; premium economy for flights 6+ hours; business class requires VP approval
- **Rental cars**: Mid-size category or below; SUV allowed for groups of 3+
- **Ride-sharing (Uber/Lyft)**: Standard or XL only; no luxury tier
- **Mileage**: Reimbursed at the IRS standard rate (currently $0.67/mile)
- **Parking**: Actual costs with receipt; airport long-term parking preferred over daily rates for trips over 3 days
- **Tolls**: Actual costs with receipt or E-ZPass statement

### 4.3 Travel — Lodging

| Location Tier | Nightly Limit |
|--------------|---------------|
| Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, DC, LA, Chicago, Boston) | $250/night |
| Tier 2 cities (other major metro areas) | $175/night |
| All other locations | $125/night |

Employees should use the company's preferred hotel booking platform (currently Navan) to access negotiated corporate rates. Airbnb is permitted for stays of 5+ nights with manager approval.

### 4.4 Communication

- International roaming charges: Reimbursable up to $15/day with manager notification
- Wi-Fi on flights: Reimbursable for flights over 2 hours when work is required

### 4.5 Professional Development

- Conference registration: Up to $2,000/year with director approval
- Books and online courses: Up to $500/year with manager approval
- Professional association membership: Up to $300/year

## 5. Expense Report Submission

### 5.1 Deadlines

- Expense reports must be submitted within **30 days** of incurring the expense
- Travel-related expense reports must be submitted within **14 days** of the trip's end date
- Expenses submitted after 60 days may be denied at the discretion of Finance

### 5.2 Required Documentation

All expense reports must include:
- Date of expense
- Business purpose description
- Names of all attendees (for meals/entertainment)
- Original receipt (photo or scan accepted)
- Foreign currency conversion rate (for international expenses)

Receipts are required for all expenses over **$25**. For expenses under $25, a written description of the expense is sufficient.

### 5.3 Submission Process

1. Log in to the expense management system (currently Expensify)
2. Create a new report and categorize each expense
3. Attach receipt photos or scans
4. Add business justification notes where required
5. Submit for manager approval
6. Finance reviews and processes approved reports

## 6. Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expense reports are reimbursed via direct deposit within **10 business days** of final approval. International reimbursements may take up to 15 business days.

## 7. Corporate Credit Cards

### 7.1 Eligibility

Corporate credit cards are provided to employees who travel frequently (4+ trips per quarter) or regularly entertain clients. Cards must be requested through Finance and approved by the employee's VP.

### 7.2 Card Usage

- Corporate cards are for business expenses only — personal use is strictly prohibited
- All corporate card transactions must still be submitted via expense report
- Lost or stolen cards must be reported to Finance immediately at finance@meridiantech.com

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses are not reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment or leisure activities
- Traffic violations, parking tickets, or towing charges
- Clothing or personal grooming
- Expenses for family members or companions
- Gym memberships or personal fitness
- Political contributions
- Gifts exceeding $50 per recipient per year
- First-class air travel without CFO approval
- Minibar charges at hotels
- Personal phone upgrade costs

## 9. Policy Violations

Submitting fraudulent, inflated, or unsupported expense claims is a serious offense that may result in:
- Denial of the expense claim
- Repayment of previously reimbursed fraudulent amounts
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal action in cases of intentional fraud
