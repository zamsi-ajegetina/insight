# New Employee Onboarding Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Applies To:** All new hires at Meridian Technologies, Inc.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the onboarding process for new employees at Meridian Technologies. A structured onboarding experience ensures new hires are set up for success with the tools, knowledge, and connections they need to thrive.

## 2. Pre-Start Preparation

### 2.1 Before Day 1

The following must be completed before the new employee's start date:

**HR Responsibilities:**
- Send welcome email with first-day logistics (building access, parking, dress code)
- Provide access to the HR portal for pre-boarding paperwork (I-9, W-4, direct deposit, benefits enrollment)
- Assign an onboarding buddy from the same department
- Schedule Week 1 orientation sessions
- Send org chart and team roster

**IT Responsibilities:**
- Provision laptop and configure with standard software suite
- Create email account and access credentials
- Set up Slack, Zoom, and project management tool accounts
- Assign phone extension (if applicable)
- Prepare security badge/access card

**Manager Responsibilities:**
- Prepare a 30-60-90 day plan with clear goals and expectations
- Identify an onboarding buddy from the team
- Block time in their calendar for Day 1 welcome and Week 1 check-ins
- Notify the team of the new hire's start date and role

## 3. Week 1: Orientation

### 3.1 Day 1

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00 AM | Meet manager at reception; building tour |
| 9:30 AM | IT setup: laptop, badges, system access |
| 10:30 AM | HR orientation: company overview, policies, benefits overview |
| 12:00 PM | Welcome lunch with team (company-sponsored) |
| 1:00 PM | Meet with manager: role overview, 30-60-90 day plan |
| 2:30 PM | Meet onboarding buddy; workspace setup |
| 3:30 PM | Self-paced: Review employee handbook, explore HR portal |
| 4:30 PM | Day 1 wrap-up with manager |

### 3.2 Days 2–5

- Complete mandatory training modules (see Section 4)
- Shadow team members on key workflows
- Attend department overview sessions
- 1:1 meetings with key stakeholders identified by manager
- Set up development environment (for engineering roles)
- Review current projects and documentation

## 4. Mandatory Training

All new employees must complete the following training within their first **30 days**:

| Training Module | Duration | Deadline |
|----------------|----------|----------|
| Company Culture & Values | 1 hour | Day 2 |
| Information Security Awareness | 2 hours | Day 3 |
| Data Privacy & Compliance | 1.5 hours | Day 5 |
| Anti-Harassment & Inclusion | 1.5 hours | Week 2 |
| Code of Conduct Acknowledgment | 30 min | Week 1 |
| Emergency Procedures | 30 min | Week 1 |
| Benefits Enrollment Workshop | 1 hour | Week 2 |
| Role-Specific Training | Varies | By Day 30 |

Training is delivered through the company's LMS (Learning Management System) and must be completed by the specified deadlines. Failure to complete mandatory training within 30 days may delay the end of the probationary period.

## 5. Onboarding Buddy Program

### 5.1 Buddy Role

Every new hire is assigned an onboarding buddy — an experienced colleague from the same or adjacent team. The buddy serves as:
- An informal resource for questions about culture, tools, and processes
- A guide for navigating office logistics and social norms
- A friendly connection to reduce first-week anxiety

### 5.2 Buddy Responsibilities

- Check in with the new hire **daily** during Week 1
- Have a **weekly 30-minute** coffee chat for the first 3 months
- Introduce the new hire to colleagues across departments
- Flag any concerns to the manager or HR proactively

### 5.3 Buddy Selection

Buddies are selected based on:
- At least 6 months of tenure at Meridian Technologies
- Strong performance and positive attitude
- Willingness to participate (buddying is voluntary)
- Proximity to the new hire's role (same or adjacent team preferred)

## 6. Equipment and Resources

### 6.1 Standard Equipment Package

All new employees receive:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro for engineering, Dell XPS for all others)
- Wireless mouse and keyboard
- Laptop stand or docking station
- Company-branded welcome kit (notebook, water bottle, t-shirt, stickers)

### 6.2 Additional Equipment

Additional equipment may be requested through IT:
- External monitor (27" — available for all employees)
- Standing desk converter (with ergonomic assessment)
- Noise-canceling headset (for roles with frequent video calls)
- Specialized software licenses (with manager approval)

### 6.3 Remote Setup

Remote employees receive their equipment via courier before their start date and are eligible for the $500 home office stipend (see Remote Work Policy, Section 4.2) after 6 months.

## 7. Probationary Period

### 7.1 Duration

All new employees are subject to a **90-day probationary period** beginning on their start date.

### 7.2 Expectations During Probation

During the probationary period, employees are expected to:
- Complete all mandatory training
- Meet the goals outlined in their 30-60-90 day plan
- Demonstrate alignment with company values
- Maintain satisfactory attendance and punctuality

### 7.3 Probation Reviews

- **30-day check-in**: Informal discussion about progress and adjustment
- **60-day review**: Formal review against 30-60-90 day plan goals
- **90-day evaluation**: Final probationary review; decision to confirm employment, extend probation (up to 30 additional days), or terminate

### 7.4 PTO During Probation

Employees accrue PTO from Day 1 but may only take PTO during the probationary period in 1-day increments, up to a maximum of 3 days (excluding company holidays and sick days).
